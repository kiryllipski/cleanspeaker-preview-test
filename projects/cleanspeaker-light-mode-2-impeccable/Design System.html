Design System

Tokens & primitives.

Single-accent system, light base. Numbers always render in JetBrains Mono with tabular figures. Surfaces stay flat. Gradient is reserved for primary CTAs and the active cleaning state only.

04 · Foundations

Motion

One easing curve, cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.8, 0.2, 1). Three durations. Animations always explain action; they never just decorate.

RoleDurationWhere it appears
Tap feedback120 msButton scale, row press
Transition260 msScreen change, option select
Reveal420 msResult confetti, check draw-on
Breathing orb1.4 s loopWelcome, clean active, downsell
CTA sheen3.6 s loopPrimary buttons
Ripple emit2.6 s loopClean active, sound test
05 · Components

Buttons

Primary uses the gradient + sheen treatment. Accent is flat for high-frequency use. Ghost is reserved for tertiary actions.

06 · Components

Cards & Rows

Cards are flat with hairline borders. Option rows snap to dark fill when active, with a check on the right.

07 · Components

Feedback & State

Step indicators, ring progress, plan cards. Numbers always render in mono.

08 · Components

Visualizations

Live, animated. The orb is canvas; the waveform, dial, and ring are SVG with RAF-driven updates.

09 · Components

Chrome

Status bar and header. The consistent top of every screen.

Icons
